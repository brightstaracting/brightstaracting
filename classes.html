🎭 What We Teach

  • Stage Speech — speaking clearly, confidently, and expressively.
  • Stage Movement — body awareness, posture, and coordination.
  • Acting Games — developing imagination and teamwork.
  • Improvisation & Character Work — building creativity and confidence.
  • Acting for Camera — understanding presence and performance on screen.
